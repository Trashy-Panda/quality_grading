      How these rankings work

      Each team result earns a rating from its placing, scaled by field size, how recent the contest is, the contest's weight, and the strength of the field (the average rating of the top ten teams there). The solver iterates until field strengths and team ratings agree, then rates each school by the average of its five best results in the selected season, using only that team's own real results this season — no cross-season blending. Teams need enough real contests this season to be numerically ranked; thinner résumés are shown separately with their actual results instead of an estimated rating. Movement compares today's board against the board before the most recent contest; NEW marks a school's first appearance.
