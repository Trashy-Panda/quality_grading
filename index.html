How scoring works — 10 pts per carcass

  • 10 Exact match
  • 9 ⅓ grade off (1 subdivision)
  • 7 ⅔ grade off (2 subdivisions)
  • 4 1 full grade off (3 subdivisions)
  • 0 More than 1 full grade off

One subdivision = one-third of a grade. High Choice → Low Prime counts as 1 subdivision off (9 pts).
